# Self-obsolescence detection

Idea seed captured 2026-06-30 for a future exploration session. The read below is tentative — confirm intent with Jesse before designing.

**Tentative read:** The system actively noticing when its *own* artifacts (notes, specs, skills, prior decisions) no longer reflect reality and flagging them for retirement — beyond passive staleness checks. Not just "this review is past its date" but "this note has been superseded and should be archived or rewritten."

**To explore:** What signals obsolescence — contradiction by newer notes, expired `review_after`, explicit superseded-by links, a decision reversed elsewhere? What action follows — flag, archive, or propose deletion (never silent)? Overlap with the Review Loop's staleness categories, and whether this is a distinct pass or an extension of it.

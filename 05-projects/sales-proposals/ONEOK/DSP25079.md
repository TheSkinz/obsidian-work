**Verification of Pass Cleanliness**

USA DeBusk uses a combination of visual indicators, data review, and physical feedback to determine the cleanliness of a decoking pass. This process is completed and reviewed side by side with the representative from the client to ensure all criteria are met.

1. During the decoking process, USA DeBusk receives real time data from numerous sensors that transmit information to the on-board operator interface. Visual indicators on the interface are represented as a spike when coke is encountered. The decoking technician has the ability to stop the decoking pig and “scrub” an exact area to remove the coke from that region.
2. The decoking technician constantly monitors the effluent returns to observe the amount of “Black” or decoking fines that are entrained within the return effluent decoking stream. As the pass becomes clean over time the visual “Black” in the stream goes to an unnoticeable amount.
3. Throughout the bi-directional pigging process used by USA DeBusk, the decoking pigs are subjected to repeat encounters with the coke that is adhered to the internal walls of the tube / pipe. This “back and forth” creates tracking and visual and physical markers on the decoking pigs. The decoking technician monitors the condition of the pigs and makes adjustments to size of the decoking pigs and the selection of decoking appendages to ensure 100% removal of the coke.
4. When the operators interface no longer indicates the presence of any fouling, the effluent water contains no “Black” and the pig indicates no tracking or remaining discoloration, the USA DeBusk shift leader will ask the client representative to come and observe first-hand the final gauge foam run, The final foam will be line size or slightly over to ensure 100% contact with the pass wall. The foam itself is constructed from a soft impressionable foam, to ensure it marks, grooves or deforms if it encounters any remaining coke. The final foam is reviewed jointly with the client and the client signs off on the cleanliness of the pass once the criteria is met.

The stepped approached outlined above has proven very effective in the field and yielded successful signs offs from all of our clients. The above system has also proven highly effective in gathering smart pigging data on the first attempt.

Design 900°F / 1050 PSIG, hydrotest 2110 PSIG; drawing notes require immediate post-hydro drain (chloride/SRB corrosion risk on 347 SS). Soda ash required per Excel (1 bag conv / 1 bag rad / 8 tank, 50-lb bags — customer scope, 347 SS passivation). Vent/drain bosses, all blinded 900#: conv 2× 3" + 2× 1½", radiant 1× 3". Isolation notes from field markup: 8"-900# blind on top of D-2479; blind on bottom shell side of X-2487.

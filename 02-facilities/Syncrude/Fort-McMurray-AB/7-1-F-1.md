## Field Notes

### CND24002 — April 2024 TA

Source: `Job_24002_Syncrude_Fort_McMurray_71_F1_Heater_Job_Report.pdf` (Jesse-supplied 2026-07-23). Rig-in
April 5, 2024. Contact Sean Joyce. Equipment: TriMax 3, TriMax 6, Support 3, Support 6, 2 crew trucks, 16
launchers, crew of 13. PM Marshall Douglas.

- **Config:** 8 coils pigged separately — inlet launcher at the control-valve station, outlet launcher at
  the radiant outlets. TriMax 6 ran passes 1/2/3/7/8 (with rig-overs between); TriMax 3 ran passes 4/5/6
  (triple mode).
- **Filtration: none** — drained to oily-water sewer (5' from rig). Water supply: hydrant. Dewater: 900 cfm
  compressor / 8" swab.
- **Coke analysis:** all 8 coils lightly-to-moderately fouled, heaviest/hardest deposits in the **last 3
  radiant tubes at the outlet (tubes 29, 30, 31)** — smart pig found minimal fouling there, "typical of
  previous decokes."
- **Delays:** de-inventory failure (each coil full of resid on arrival → hours of standby to fill/flush/
  remove product before cleaning) and TIs left in the coils (each coil filled/flushed/dewatered before the
  plant could pull TIs from the convection-to-radiant crossovers). Total standby 36 hrs.
- **Decoking stats:** avg cleaning duration (coils 1–8) 24 hrs; 62 TC pigs used (8 per coil); initial pig 6"
  TC, final 6.25" TC. Pigs used: 6" ×24 (13 foam/11 TC), 6.125" ×4 TC, 6.25" ×39 (4 foam/35 TC), 6.5" ×8
  (6 TC/2 swab), 8.5" ×8 (6 TC/2 swab). Quest smart pig all 8 coils. Full before/after flow-test table (all
  8 passes) is in the source PDF.
- **Geometry confirmation:** this report's coil-data table + section drawings are the source that verified
  the heater's tube geometry (16 conv + 31 rad = 47 tubes, 2,311 ft total, 8 coils) — see Tube Geometry.

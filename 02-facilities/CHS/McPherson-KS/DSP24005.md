## Verification of Pass Cleanliness — method as quoted
Real-time sensor data to the on-board operator interface, with coke encounters showing as spikes and the
technician able to stop and scrub a specific area · continuous monitoring of effluent returns for
entrained decoking fines until "black" becomes unnoticeable · bi-directional pigging producing tracking
and physical markers on the pigs, with pig size and appendage selection adjusted accordingly · final
gauge foam run observed first-hand by the client representative.

This method statement is the basis for answering CHS's fouling-versus-cleanliness correlation question.

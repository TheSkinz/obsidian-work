## Key Technical Findings

**Design data was wrong on two of nine heaters.** [[HF-0012]] Coker measured 3.7" against a 3.232"
design ID, confirmed across six independent shifts. [[HP-0006]] measured 6.0" by smart pig against a
5.761" design ID. Both understate bore. Treat CHS-supplied technical data as estimating input only.

**[[HP-0003]] stuck pig.** The 3.826" convection to 4.813" radiant step is a 4"→5" reducer. A pig hung
there overnight 04-01 and was still stuck at shift end. [[HP-0025]] has the same two-bore feature and
was pigged without incident because it was planned for — that contrast is the lesson.

**[[HF-0012]] radiant fouling is the site's signature problem.** Radiant outlet tubes extremely dirty,
pigs sticking, one pass unable to be bought off across a full shift. Hell Razors were required; a
TC-only plan will not clear it.

> [!warning] Step change in bore — 3.826" convection to 4.813" radiant
> This is the defining feature of the heater and it caused the job's only stuck pig. The card's
> convection/radiant ID mismatch is a **4"→5" reducer** in the flow path. Any pig sized for the radiant
> will not pass the convection, and a pig sized for the convection will under-clean the radiant.

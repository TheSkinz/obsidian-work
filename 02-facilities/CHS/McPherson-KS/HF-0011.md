## Field Notes

### USA25025 — 2025 Plant-Wide TA

**Tickets:** 9163 · 9165–9172 · 8792–8796. Rig-in 1pm–7pm 03-29 (9163).

**Shift narrative (receipts 8792–8796):** Pigged by section, matching the four-bore coil. Pass 1 & 3
first — polishing the 6", 10" and 8" sections, running 6.25" TC/HR/Foam, 8.25" TC and 10.25" TC with
12" swabs to dewater. Pass 3 reached smart-pig readiness ahead of Pass 1, which was still polishing its
6" section into the following shift. Launchers were then moved to Pass 2 & 4 and the 8" and 10" sections
pigged before smart pigging. Final rig-out April 5–6: 10" and 6" launchers removed, press cleaned, tanks
washed, hoses picked up.

> [!important] Smart pig could not get clean data on first attempts
> On the overnight smart-pig run (8793), "air and particulates prevented smart pig from obtaining
> sufficient data." All sections were run with several attempts before data was bought off around shift
> change. A four-bore vacuum coil entrains air at the size transitions — budget repeat smart-pig runs
> here rather than a single pass.

> [!warning] Ticket 8796 — 8 hrs rig-out unbilled (INTERNAL)
> The 04-05 night rig-out shift (8796, 8 hrs rig-out + support + crew truck) is signed by the customer
> but has no entry in the billing workbook — the NIGHT (16) tab is empty. Eight hours of documented
> TriMax 1 rig-out went unbilled. Task Durations reflects the **billed** figure to stay tied to the
> auditable record; the field-documented total is 8 hrs higher. See [[USA25025-job-record]].

**Estimating note:** 5,004' across four bores with filtration and soda-ash premix took 96 pigging hours
plus 18 smart-pig hours at routine fouling, pigged as two pass-pairs (1 & 3, then 2 & 4). Exclude the
51 idle hours when using this job to estimate.

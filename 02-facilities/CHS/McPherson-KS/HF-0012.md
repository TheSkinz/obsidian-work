## Field Notes

### USA25025 — 2025 Plant-Wide TA

**Progression by cell.** Cells 2 & 3 first set of passes ran 3.05"→3.8" TC; cells 1 & 2 second set ran
3.1"→3.8" with Hell Razors introduced for the fouled sections. Started each set at line size and stepped
oversize. Largest pig achieved 4.1".

> [!warning] Radiant section is the problem area
> - 9158 (3/26 night): **"Radiant outlet tubes extremely dirty. Pigs sticking in Radiant."**
> - 9162 (3/28 night): **"Pass 2 still has fouling in Radiant section."** Passes 1 & 3 could not be
>   bought off until day shift.
> - 9159 (3/27): 2 hrs standby to dislodge pigs from radiant tubes.
>
> Convection cleaned normally. All of the difficulty on this heater was radiant-side, and it is the
> single most repeatable finding for future Coker bids here.

**Verification:** smart pig inspected all three coils 3/26, CHS **bought off at 5pm**; second smart pig
run 3/28 for the remaining passes.

**Estimating note:** 12,036' of 3.7" coil at routine fouling took 85 allocated pigging hours (94
receipt wall-clock incl. holds) plus 10.5 smart-pig hours in triple mode. Budget radiant-section
recovery time explicitly — pigs stuck in the radiant on at least two shifts. Hell Razors were needed;
a TC-only pig plan will not clear this radiant. Prep note (quote): **blast bottom tubes prior to
decoke**.

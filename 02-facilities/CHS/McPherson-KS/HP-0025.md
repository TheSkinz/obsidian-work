## Field Notes

### USA25025 — 2025 Plant-Wide TA

**Sequence:** rig-in 6 hrs connecting hoses to launchers on temp piping; pigged passes 1 & 2 through
convection and radiant, running oversize at shift change with smart piggers standing by to inspect
immediately (9298). Night shift polished convection and radiant passes prior to inspection, smart pigged
both, **data bought off at 1am**, then rigged out and moved to HP-0002/0003/0006 (9299).

**Estimating note:** 2,328' turned in a single 24-hour cycle including rig-in, smart pig and rig-out.
The two-bore layout costs pig inventory (two size families) but not time when planned for. Contrast with
[[HP-0003]], where the same feature was not planned around.

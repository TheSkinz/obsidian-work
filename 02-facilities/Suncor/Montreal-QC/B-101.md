# B-101 Vacuum Feed Heater — Suncor Montreal, QC

> ⚠ MIXED METALLURGY — Convection is A335 Gr P5 (5Cr-½Mo); Radiant is A335 Gr P9 (9Cr-1Mo), per-section per the BOM. Neither section is stainless, so the default stainless→soda-ash expectation does not apply on metallurgy. But the Suncor rep's decoke spec (UD1 "water/soda ash" temp-spool spec) calls for soda ash across all five heaters — including carbon-steel B-151 — regardless of tube metallurgy. **This is a customer-instruction override, not a USADeBusk requirement** (Jesse 2026-07-23): soda ash / low-chloride water is always customer scope (USADeBusk does not provide or mix it), and the governing method is whatever the customer rep's RFQ/bid instructions specify. Follow their instructions — read the RFQ for this facility's passivation method before scoping.
